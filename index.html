01 / How long does a deployment take, from container arrival to power?

First deployment typically requires approximately 8 hours with a team of four people. This includes site preparation, ground levelling using the supplied support legs, rail alignment and system commissioning.

After the one-time setup, the system can be deployed or retracted in approximately 25 minutes through a fully automated process. The unit can then be relocated and redeployed repeatedly without civil works, concrete foundations or permanent infrastructure.

02 / What’s the lead time from order to power on site?

Depending on destination and project scope, typical lead time ranges from 10–20 weeks from contract signature to a fully operational system on site. This includes production, ocean or road transport, customs handling and final commissioning by a local EPC partner.

Multi-unit orders benefit from parallel production scheduling, enabling faster deployment of larger fleets and phased project roll-outs.

03 / Who handles installation, service and maintenance in-country?

Saphira operates through a partner-based model. Local EPC, installation and service partners manage customer relationships, deployment, commissioning and field support within their respective markets.

Saphira Systems provides the SAPHIRA CORE unit, partner training, technical support and access to a spare-parts supply chain.

This approach ensures local responsiveness and service capability while maintaining direct access to the manufacturer’s technical expertise and support network.

04 / What warranty and asset lifetime do you offer?

System warranty: 2-year warranty on the structural and mechanical components of the system. PV modules, inverters and other electrical components are covered by the respective manufacturer warranties.

Designed lifetime: 25 years for the asset as a whole, assuming standard service intervals.

05 / In which climates can Saphira Core operate?

Operating envelope: −30 °C to +70 °C, wind resistance up to 89 km/h (Bft 9) deployed. Wind sensor with auto-retract above threshold.

06 / Does the unit work as a standalone or only hybrid with diesel / BESS?

Both. The EMS supports grid-forming and grid-following modes and parallel operation with diesel gensets and BESS — but the unit is hybrid-ready, not hybrid-required.

Pure PV daytime offset, PV + BESS for 24/7 off-grid, or PV + BESS + diesel for mission-critical sites. Microgrid integration and grid feed-in are both supported.

07 / How do shipping, customs and country compliance work?

Saphira Core ships in a standard 20 ft HC container — handled by any port, road or rail logistics chain. CSC-certified for international ocean transport.

CE-certified, TÜV Süd tested. In-country electrical conformity (grid code, earthing, protection) is handled by the local EPC partner against their national standards.

08 / What financing or leasing models are available?

SAPHIRA CORE systems are typically purchased directly through authorised partners and distributors.

While Saphira Systems does not currently provide financing or leasing services, we support customers and financing institutions with comprehensive project documentation, energy yield assessments, ROI calculations and bankable financial models to facilitate investment and financing decisions.
